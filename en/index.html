Use reactions in chats ✅

When someone posts a message like “Who can join tomorrow?” in a group chat, instead of replying with messages like “Yes”, “Me” or “Ok”, it’s much better to react with an emoji. 😊

📩 Why it's better:
  • The person asking sees who reacted.
  • You avoid sending unnecessary notifications to the whole group.
  • The chat stays cleaner and more readable.
  • It's faster and less disruptive.

Short replies like “Yes” trigger notifications for everyone and bury the original message. 😵

A simple reaction (👍, ✅, 🙋‍♀️...) makes things clear — and only notifies the person who needs to know. 💡

❌ Don’t do this:

Anna: Who can come to the meeting tomorrow?
Me
Yes
Ok
Count me in

✅ Do this instead:

Anna: Who can come to the meeting tomorrow?
👍 🙋‍♀️ ✅

Let’s make group chats more useful and less noisy. 🙏
