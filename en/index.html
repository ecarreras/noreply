Use reactions in chats ✅

When someone writes a message in a group, like “Who can join tomorrow?”, instead of replying with messages like “Yes”, “Me” or “Ok”, it's much better to react with an emoji. 😊

📩 Advantages:
  • The person who asked sees who reacted.
  • You avoid sending unnecessary notifications to the whole group.
  • The chat stays much cleaner.
  • It’s quicker and less disruptive.

Short replies like “Yes” send a notification to everyone and bury the original message. 😵

With a simple reaction (👍, ✅, 🙋‍♀️...), the message is clear, and only notifies the one who needs it. 💡

❌ Don’t do this:

AnnaWho can come to the meeting tomorrow?
JoanMe
LaiaYes
PereOk
MartaMe too

✅ Do this instead:

AnnaWho can come to the meeting tomorrow?
👍2
🙋‍♀️1
✅1

Let’s make group chats more useful and less noisy. 🙏
